About the App

HeartShare is a real-time heart rate sharing app built for athletes and coaches. It lets you broadcast your live pulse from a wide range of devices to a shared session, so coaches can monitor intensity, adjust workouts on the fly, and keep everyone training in the right zone: whether you're in the gym, on the track, or remote.

Features

Live Heart Rate Sharing

Broadcast your heart rate in real time so coaches and teammates can follow your intensity during a workout.

Coach View (No Payment Required)

Coaches can join a session with a simple code and monitor athletes live—no subscription needed.

Multi-Device Connectivity

Connect with a wide range of heart-rate devices and wearables, so you can train with what you already have.

Data Collection

HeartShare collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Account information: Email address or sign-in identifier (Authorization is required to restore your subscription across devices.)
  • Subscription status: Used to verify and restore access to paid features.
  • Advertising data: AdMob may collect device identifiers and basic device information to display and measure ads in the free version.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is used to provide app functionality, manage subscriptions, and display ads.
